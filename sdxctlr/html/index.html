AtlanticWave SDX

The AtlanticWave/SDX is a distributed research and education network Internet exchange point (IXP)


Use cases for this IXP

Distribution of terabytes of data from new American telescopes located in the Atacama Desert of Chile back to the US
This traffic is bursty in nature, only during certain hours, and will require near-full utilization of the anticipated links between Atlanta, Miami, São Paulo and Chile
